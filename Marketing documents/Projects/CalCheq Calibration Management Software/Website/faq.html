Getting Started

Features

How does the compliance certificate generation work?

After a calibration is recorded, CalCheq auto-generates AS/NZS ISO 17025–aligned certificates with one click. Include as-found readings, as-left readings, measurement uncertainty (if entered by your calibrator), technician sign-off, traceability information, and calibration date. Export as PDF, email to your customer, or archive in CalCheq for audit retrieval. You can also customize certificate templates per customer.

Can technicians record calibrations on mobile?

Yes. The mobile field app works on iPhone and Android, online and offline. Technicians log readings in real time on the shop floor, photograph the instrument or calibrator display, and upload calibrator data (Beamex/Fluke CSV). Data syncs to the dashboard when reconnected. Perfect for remote sites and multi-location plants.

What does "criticality ranking" do and why do I need it?

Criticality ranking assigns Red (safety-critical), Yellow (process-critical), or Green (reference) labels to each instrument based on its role in your process and SIS program. This helps you: prioritise maintenance on the instruments that matter most, allocate technician time effectively, and demonstrate risk-based compliance to auditors (especially for IEC 61511). It's a best practice that CalCheq automates.

How do predictive degradation alerts work?

CalCheq learns from your historical calibration data over time. If an instrument shows a trend toward exceeding tolerance (e.g., accuracy drifting over multiple calibrations), we flag it with an alert before it fails. This reduces emergency repairs and process downtime. Red (safety-critical) instruments get the highest alert priority. Available on Professional and Enterprise plans.

Can I manage multiple sites in one account?

Yes. Professional and Enterprise plans include multi-site management. Create separate sites, assign instruments to each, set site-specific calibration intervals, and manage team permissions by role and location. Corporate dashboards roll up compliance status across all sites. Enterprise customers also get a dedicated account manager and custom workflows per site.

Compliance & Standards

Is CalCheq AS/NZS ISO 17025 compliant?

CalCheq helps you comply with AS/NZS ISO 17025 by automating calibration scheduling, recording, traceability, and certificate generation. Our certificates include all required elements (as-found/as-left readings, measurement uncertainty, technician identification, date, scope). We're not an accrediting body, but NATA-accredited labs use CalCheq. Bring your process documentation to us; we'll help you align it with the standard.

Can CalCheq help with NATA accreditation?

NATA accreditation requires strict calibration controls, traceability, and documented procedures. CalCheq supports these by providing audit-ready records, automated scheduling, and certificate generation aligned with ISO 17025. You'll still need to document your QMS and quality procedures, but CalCheq eliminates the manual tracking that typically causes audit failures. Several NATA-accredited labs in Australia use CalCheq.

What's the connection to IEC 61511 (Safety Instrumented Systems)?

IEC 61511 requires rigorous proof that SIS instruments (pressure transmitters, shut-off valves, emergency stop buttons) are calibrated and functioning within tolerance. Criticality ranking in CalCheq automates Red (SIS-critical) instrument identification, predicts degradation, and generates compliance certificates proving when each device was last calibrated. This is critical for major hazard facilities (oil, gas, chemical plants).

What audit documentation does CalCheq produce?

Audit-ready reports include: calibration history (all as-found/as-left readings), compliance status (overdue instruments, missed intervals), technician sign-offs, certificate archive, multi-site rollup, and criticality status by instrument. All timestamped with full traceability. Export as PDF or keep online. Designed specifically for ISO 17025, NATA, and IEC 61511 audits.

Data & Security

Where is my data hosted?

CalCheq data is hosted on Australian servers (Sydney region) with redundant backups in compliance with Australian Privacy Principles. We're SOC 2 Type II certified and encrypt all data in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). If you have specific data residency or security requirements, Enterprise customers can arrange on-premise deployment or private cloud hosting.

How often is my data backed up?

Daily automated backups, with point-in-time recovery up to 30 days. We also maintain real-time replication across multiple data centres. In the unlikely event of a disaster, we can restore your complete database within 2 hours. Enterprise customers get 90-day backup retention and custom RPO/RTO agreements.

Who owns my data?

You do. CalCheq is your data processor, not your data owner. You can export all your data at any time as CSV or PDF. If you cancel, we'll provide a full data export and then delete your account after 90 days. We never sell, share, or use your calibration data for any other purpose.
