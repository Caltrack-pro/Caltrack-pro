Criticality Ranking — How It Works
LevelInstrument TypeDashboard Priority
Red — SIS Safety instrumented systems, trip instruments. Plant shutdown on failure. Always top of overdue list. Critical alert sent immediately on overdue.
Yellow — Controller Control instruments affecting product quality, throughput, or equipment. Second priority. Alert sent at 80% of calibration interval.
Green — Indication Indication-only. No control or trip function. Lowest priority. Alert sent at due date.
