## About CalCheq
CalCheq is an industrial calibration management platform with built-in analytics. It replaces spreadsheets, legacy CMMS attachments, and fragmented calibration tracking with a unified system.

**Core capabilities:**
- Calibration management and scheduling
- Due date control and overdue visibility
- Pass/fail automation
- Audit readiness and compliance
- Calibration history and certificate access
- Drift trending and analytics
- Preventative and predictive maintenance insights
- Smarter maintenance decision-making

**Origin story:** Built by someone who lived the pain — working in industrial sites where calibration was tracked in spreadsheets, buried in CMMS attachments, and invisible to decision-makers.

**Target audience:** Instrumentation technicians, E&I supervisors, reliability engineers, maintenance planners, plant managers, compliance stakeholders, operations leaders — primarily in mining, oil & gas, power generation, water treatment, manufacturing, and heavy industry.

**Competitors to position against:** Spreadsheets, CMMS attachment workflows, legacy calibration systems, generic asset management tools, reactive maintenance culture.
