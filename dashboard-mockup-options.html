Proposed Tab Structure — What Goes Where

Right now there's overlap between Schedule and Reports (both show overdue instruments).
Here's a proposal to give each tab a clear, non-overlapping purpose.

🏠 Dashboard
Purpose: "What do I need to know right now?"

The command centre. Shows KPIs, instrument health (the new donut/bar), action items (overdue, pending, drift alerts), and area compliance. No tables of individual instruments — those belong in other tabs. The dashboard answers three questions: Am I compliant? What's drifting? What needs my attention today?

Sections: KPI cards → Instrument Health chart → Action summary cards → Area compliance bars → Upcoming 7 days → Quick Actions

🔧 Instruments
Purpose: "Find, view, or edit any instrument"

The register. Searchable, filterable, sortable list of all instruments. This is the CRUD page — add, edit, view details, see calibration history. Stays as-is; the table layout fix already shipped. No predictive content here — this is the master data view.

Unchanged. List → Detail → Edit / Calibrate / Audit Trail

📅 Schedule
Purpose: "What needs doing, and when?"

The work planner. This is where technicians and planners live. Four tabs focused on actionable work:

Overdue
Past due date. Sorted by days overdue (worst first). Action: calibrate now.
Due Soon
Due within 14 days. Plan these into this week's work.
Repeat Failures
2+ consecutive as-found fails. These need investigation, not just recalibration.
Drift Alerts
Predicted to fail before next due date. This is the predictive maintenance tab. Make it the default?

Idea: Rename "Schedule" to "Schedule & Health" or "Maintenance" to signal this isn't just a calendar — it includes predictive health intelligence.

📋 Calibrations
Purpose: "Manage the approval workflow and see recent activity"

The workflow hub. Two tabs: Pending Approvals (records submitted, awaiting supervisor review) and Activity Log (recent calibration records across the site). This is the supervisor's inbox. Stays as-is.

Unchanged. Pending Approvals (with badge count) → Activity Log

📄 Reports
Purpose: "Generate documents for audits, managers, and compliance records"

The export centre. This should be focused on generating output — PDFs, CSVs, compliance packs. Currently it duplicates the Schedule page's data as tables. Proposed change: refocus on exportable outputs:

Compliance Report
Full site compliance summary — all instruments, due dates, status. PDF export for auditors.
Calibration History
Search by tag → view all records → export PDF certificate or multi-record report.
Overdue Register
Exportable list of all overdue instruments with last calibration details. CSV + PDF.
Health & Drift Report (NEW)
Predictive health summary — instruments at risk, drift trends, recommended interval changes. Your premium feature.
⚙️ Settings
Purpose: "Manage your site, team, and account"

Unchanged. Site info, profile, password, team members. Future: billing tab for Stripe integration.

🆘 Support
Purpose: "Get help using CalCheq"

Unchanged. FAQ accordion, tutorial placeholders (replace with real docs or remove "Coming soon"), contact email.
