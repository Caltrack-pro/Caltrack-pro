The 4-Step Setup Process

Get your entire calibration program live faster than you thought possible.

1

Import

Upload your instrument data via CSV, Excel, or our guided import wizard. Map your columns, validate data, and have your instrument register loaded in minutes. Supports data from MEX, SAP, Maximo, and manual spreadsheets.

2

Configure

Set calibration intervals, define criticality levels, assign team members to instruments, and configure alert thresholds. Use our templates for common equipment types or customise per-instrument. No technical knowledge required.

3

Record

Log calibrations in real-time from the dashboard or via mobile field app. Attach certificates, record as/found and as/left readings, upload calibrator data (Beamex, Fluke CSV), and generate automated compliance evidence instantly.

4

Report

Run compliance reports, export certificates aligned with AS/NZS ISO 17025, track overdue instruments, and monitor criticality status across your site. Scheduled reporting and dashboard alerts keep everyone informed.

Feature Deep-Dive

Six core capabilities that set CalCheq apart.

🔴

Instrument Criticality Ranking

Automatically assign Red (safety-critical SIS), Yellow (process-critical), or Green (reference) ratings based on your site's risk matrix. Prioritise maintenance and compliance audits on the instruments that matter most. Critical for IEC 61511 and SIS programs.

📊

Real-Time Calibration Dashboard

See at a glance: overdue instruments, upcoming calibrations, compliance status by criticality, technician workload, and historical trends. Customizable widgets let each role see what they need. Mobile-responsive for shop floor access.

✓

Compliance Certificate Generation

Auto-generate AS/NZS ISO 17025–aligned calibration certificates in seconds. Include as-found/as-left readings, measurement uncertainty, technician sign-off, and full traceability. Export as PDF or archive digitally. NATA-ready format.

⚠️

Predictive Degradation Alerts

CalCheq learns from your historical calibration data and flags instruments showing degradation trends before they exceed tolerance. Reduce emergency repairs and keep your process stable. Red instruments get highest alert priority.

🏢

Multi-Site Management

Manage calibration programs across multiple processing plants from a single dashboard. Site-specific permissions, separate calibration schedules, and consolidated compliance reporting. Perfect for corporate groups and mining networks.

📱

Field Data Entry & Calibrator Integration

Technicians record calibrations on mobile, even offline. Push data when connected. Direct integration with Beamex and Fluke CSV output lets you upload calibrator readings directly. No manual transcription, zero data-entry errors.
